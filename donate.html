Where Your Money Goes

100% to Make-A-Wish Michigan.

Every dollar raised through The Grateful Team flows directly to Make-A-Wish Michigan — a nonprofit that grants life-changing wishes to Michigan children fighting critical illnesses. We don't keep a cut. There are no admin fees on our end. You donate; kids get wishes.

Make-A-Wish Michigan covers everything — travel, accommodations, experiences — so the families pay nothing. Your donation buys a kid a moment they'll remember for the rest of their life.

Since 2023, our team of riders has raised $265,287 for wish kids across Michigan. We're not slowing down.
