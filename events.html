Zoo de Mack 2026 — our season kickoff.

Saturday, May 16, 2026

Zoo de Mack 2026

📍The Highlands (Point A) → Mackinaw City finish line fiesta
🚲~51 miles, point-to-point
🏨Most of the team stays at Baymont by Wyndham, Mackinaw City
🎉Post-ride party at The Crossings, then ferry to Mackinac Island for the night

Our official-unofficial kickoff to the season: a ~51-mile ride across the top of the Lower Peninsula, finish line fiesta in Mackinaw City, and the whole crew rolling over to Mackinac Island for the evening. Return ferries run until midnight.
