Stuff That Works

Gear Recommendations.

Picks from veteran TGT riders. No affiliate links, no kickbacks — just stuff we've actually used on the road.

Your saddle is a personal relationship; consider this a starting point, not a commandment. If something here doesn't work for you, the team has tried plenty of alternatives — ask in the group chat.

Solid entry-level bib that won't punish your wallet or your sit bones. Great if you're new to long days and not ready to drop premium money on something you'll find out you love or hate.

pearlizumi.com →
Castelli Endurance 3 Bib Shorts$$

The reliable mid-tier workhorse. Castelli's pad is famously comfortable, and these hold up to repeat wash cycles all summer long.

Rapha Pro Team Bib Shorts$$$

If you ride enough miles to know what you want, Rapha's Pro Team line is the move. Compressive, supportive, and the chamois holds up over multi-day rides.
